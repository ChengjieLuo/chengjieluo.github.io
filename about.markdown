I am a theoretical physicist at the interface of non-equilibrium statistical mechanics, soft matter, and biological physics. By combining analytical theory with computer simulations, I investigate how microscopic interactions — short-range and long-range, pairwise and higher-order, passive and chemically active — govern the macroscopic organization of matter. My work spans liquid–liquid phase separation, the glass transition, and the collective dynamics of complex fluids.
